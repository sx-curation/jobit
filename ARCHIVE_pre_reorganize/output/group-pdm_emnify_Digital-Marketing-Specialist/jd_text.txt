Digital Marketing Specialist - Berlin/Germany




Your Role




As a Digital Marketing Specialist reporting to our VP of Marketing, Mia you will take full ownership of executing high-impact paid and organic campaigns across LinkedIn, Google, and Meta.




This is a hands-on, execution-focused role where you will be responsible for building, launching, and optimising campaigns that directly drive pipeline. You’ll work closely with Product Marketing and Regional Marketing teams to translate ICPs and personas into high-performing campaigns.




You’ll play a critical role in bringing paid media in-house, replacing external agency support, and helping us scale efficiently.

This role is ideal for a hungry, modern marketer who thrives on execution, experimentation, and continuous learning — not just strategy.




Why emnify




Work alongside experienced marketing leaders and high-performing teams across regions
Gain exposure to a complex and fast-growing industry (IoT) that will accelerate your learning curve
Have real ownership and visibility — your work directly impacts pipeline and revenue
Be part of a collaborative culture where even junior team members have avoice
Opportunity to experiment with the latest tools, AI capabilities, and growth tactics




Your Goals




Build, launch, and optimise paid campaigns across LinkedIn, Google Ads, and Meta
Generate high-quality MQLs and SQLs from target ICP accounts
Continuously improve campaign performance through testing, optimisation, and experimentation
Contribute to bringing paid media fully in-house and improving efficiency




Your Impact




Execute and optimise multi-channel demand generation campaigns
Own campaign performance from click to conversion (MQL → SQL)
Collaborate with Product Marketing and Regional teams to refine targeting and messaging
Provide feedback on ad creatives, landing pages, and conversion flows
Experiment with new tools, AI capabilities, and growth tactics to improve performance
Contribute to integrated campaigns across digital, events, and ABX initiatives




What We Expect




Experience in digital marketing, performance marketing, or demand generation roles

Hands-on experience with:




LinkedIn Campaign Manager
Google AdsPaid social (Meta)Strong execution mindset — you enjoy building and optimising campaigns, not just planning them
Curiosity and passion for modern marketing tools, including AI-driven optimisation
Ability to analyse performance and continuously improve results




Bonus:

Experience with HubSpot or similar CRM/marketing tools
Basic design skills (e.g. Canva)Exposure to B2B marketing or growth marketing




What Great Looks Like:




You think beyond channels and understand how digital fits into broader campaigns
You actively experiment with new tools, formats, and approaches
You can write or improve ad copy and contribute to creative direction
You take ownership of results — not just activity
