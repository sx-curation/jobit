Job Description



To further strengthen the Product Line Sensor Signal Conditioning and ASICs is looking for a Product Marketing Specialist (f/m/d) to drive the growth next Generation Automotive, Industrial and Robotics Sensor Products. 



Key Responsibilities:



Market Intelligence & Strategic Analysis:



Investigate key market trends and competitive landscape, analyzing Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Environmental, and Legal (PESTEL) factors to identify risks and opportunities
Develop and regularly review market models defining TAM/SAM/SOM using both top-down and bottom-up approaches integrated with product lifecycle models to support revenue planning
Monitor competitor activities and establish benchmarking schemes to maintain competitive positioning
Provide regular market intelligence and portfolio insights to Product Managers and key stakeholders

Product Strategy & Portfolio Management:



Support the definition of product roadmap in collaboration with Product Managers, Application Engineers, System Architects, FAE, and Regional organizations
Monitor the product portfolio continuously, identifying gaps, weaknesses, and opportunities to drive strategic actions
Develop worldwide pricing strategies with Product Managers and support the quoting process

Customer Value Proposition & Market Understanding:



Work cross-functionally to deeply understand market requirements, customer gains, and pain points across Automotive, Industrial, and Robotics sectors
Transform customer insights and application requirements into compelling differentiators and value propositions, with focus on Total Cost of Ownership
Drive business development activities to expand the product line footprint and market reach

Cross-Functional Collaboration & Stakeholder Management:



Build trusted relationships and collaborate intensively with regional business development teams, Application Engineers, and internal stakeholders to support business objectives
Represent the Product Line to internal and external partners, including exhibitions and customer meetings

Marketing Operations & Execution:



Define and execute MarCom activities and support product launches with appropriate marketing materials
Maintain regular updates of market models, lifecycle models, and planning documentation
Provide ongoing updates on product, customer, volume, and pricing data to support overall strategy, pricing initiatives, and capacity planning in alignment with key stakeholders

Highly interested in automotive, industrial and robotics industry and the willingness do understand the connection within the industry and also links to other industries
Highly motivated do gain expertise in semiconductors, especially in sensors

Strong public speaker, comfortable in front of large, senior groups, up to executive management
English business fluent. German language nice to have
Regular domestic and International travelling Worldwide (based on company travel guidelines)
Excellent skills in Microsoft Office, especially in Excel and PowerPoint
