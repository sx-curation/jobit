Millions of people across Europe are looking for better ways to build long-term wealth. We're building the platform that makes it possible.

As our Junior Lifecycle & Retention Marketing Specialist, you'll execute ad-hoc campaigns and multi-channel automations to keep our users engaged and active. Using Braze, our customer engagement platform, you'll craft personalized experiences and collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless campaign delivery. If you're excited about making investing more accessible, we'd love to hear from you.

What You'll Do


Execute and monitor email, push, and in-app campaigns with precision
Manage deadlines and dependencies across multiple campaigns and stakeholders
Build and analyze campaign performance reports using Excel and other analytics tools
Maintain thorough documentation of campaign setups and results
Conduct quality assurance to ensure accuracy in all outgoing communications
Help optimize customer segments through data analysis and actionable insights


Your professional skills and background

Must-haves


1+ year of work experience in marketing, data analytics, product, or operations
Exceptional attention to detail with a strong quality control mindset
Advanced Excel skills and experience with data analysis
Proven ability to manage multiple projects while meeting deadlines with high accuracy
Excellent communication and organizational skills
Interest in investment and financial products
Higher education


Nice-to-haves


Experience with Braze or similar customer engagement platforms
Experience with project management
Understanding of A/B testing principles
Knowledge of email marketing, mobile push notifications and in-app messaging.
