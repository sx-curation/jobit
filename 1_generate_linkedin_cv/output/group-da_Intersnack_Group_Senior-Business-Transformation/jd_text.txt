Job Description

Why This Role Matters

As a Senior Business Transformation Analyst, you will deliver transformation projects within the Transformation Management sub-department, working independently in at least one functional area. You will apply expertise in project management, process mapping, and solution design, support the implementation of Business Transformation’s methodologies and tools, and drive process improvements and IT solutions for one end-to-end process.

As part of the Supporting Functions team, you will contribute to driving projects that improve HR, financial, and internal control processes. You will help Improve efficiency and ascertain compliance in for enabling functions & processes.

Your Role at Intersnack


Execute project tasks independently, including process mapping, data analysis, and solution design, under minimal supervision
Support the implementation of Business Transformation’s methodologies and tools in collaboration with Management Units, Group Functions, and external partners
Contribute to the development of project plans, functional specifications, and change management activities
Actively participate in knowledge-sharing and training projects to build team capabilities
Adhere to Business Transformation’s standards for documentation, reporting, and stakeholder communication


Education

Job requirements

University degree in Business Administration, Science, Technology, Engineering, Computer Science, Mathematics, or a related field

Experience

Some professional experience and familiarity with project management, business analysis, or consulting

Skills


Strong problem-solving, communication, and stakeholder management skills
Ability to adapt quickly to new tasks and project requirements
Collaborative and client-focused mindset
Experience with project documentation and reporting tools
Fluency in English; additional languages are a plus


Additional Requirements


Willingness to travel approximately 4 days per week across Intersnack locations.

About Intersnack

Intersnack is a leading European producer of savory snacks, dedicated to delivering exceptional quality and creating memorable snacking experiences. As a privately held company, we prioritise long-term growth and sustainability, driving innovation while maintaining a strong commitment to environmental and social responsibility.

With a robust financial performance—achieving a turnover exceeding €4.5 billion in 2024—we continue to expand our global footprint. Our operations span over 30 countries, supported by 12 regional management units, 45 production facilities, and a diverse workforce of approximately 15,000 employees worldwide.
